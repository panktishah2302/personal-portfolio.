Who am I?

Hello there! I'm Pankti Shah, a passionate and results-driven web developer proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, Git, GitHub, and MySQL.

I recently completed my Bachelor's of Engineering from Sal College of Engineering, affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU), where I enhanced my programming skills and gained hands-on experience through a rewarding 3-month internship as a Django Developer at VNurture Technologies.

One of the highlights of my internship was leading the development of a Food Calorie Tracker. This project allowed me to apply my skills in Django, creating a user-friendly web application that enables users to track their daily food intake and monitor their calorie consumption.

Now actively seeking new opportunities to apply and expand my skills. I am eager to contribute my technical proficiency and fresh perspective to a growth-oriented team.

My goal is to apply my passion for web development and problem-solving skills to make a meaningful impact in the tech industry. If you have exciting projects or opportunities, I'd love to connect and explore potential collaborations.
